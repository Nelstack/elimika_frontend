Our Services

Everything You Need
to Succeed

From online classes to exam prep and mentorship, Elimika provides a full suite of educational services tailored for Kenyan learners.

Online Learning

Live and recorded virtual classes with interactive whiteboards and instant feedback from certified tutors.

Physical Tuition

One-on-one and small group in-person sessions at our learning centers across Nairobi and major Kenyan towns.

Holiday Revision

Intensive revision programs during school holidays. Structured, focused camps designed to boost understanding and grades.

Exam Preparation

KCPE, KCSE, and JSE-focused preparation with past papers, mock exams, timed practice, and expert review sessions.

Assignment Help

Step-by-step guidance on school assignments and projects, helping learners understand the work rather than just complete it.

Mentorship

Career guidance, study skills coaching, and life mentorship from educators who care about each learner's growth beyond academics.

Our online classes use a secure video platform with interactive whiteboards and real-time Q&A. Sessions are recorded for later review. You just need a smartphone or laptop and stable internet.
